Projects

Basketball Shooting in VR

This is my second project in my mixed Reality major in Centrale Nantes. This VR game gives the possibility to the player to shoot a ball from different positions in the court, a counter gives the score of the player. In this project, we tried to give an experience close to the real life with dimensions of the court, player, ball and positions similar to the dimensions in real life. So, the project can be a fun practising game to shooting.
Code Language & technlogies:
C#, Unity, VR tools (SteamVR, Occulus)
Supervised by:
Jean-Marie Normand
